Daniel Garibi

I'm a PhD student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Tel-Aviv University, supervised by Daniel Cohen-Or and Hadar Averbuch-Elor. I started my MA studies while completing my BSc (Summa Cum Laude) at Tel-Aviv University.

My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, and generative models. More specifically, I am interested in developing new tools for content synthesis and editing.

Currently interning at Snap Research working to extend controllability on generative models. Previously worked at Google DeepMind on personalizing generative models.
